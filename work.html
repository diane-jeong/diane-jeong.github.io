Project & Internship Experience

To be completely honest, I had no idea what I wanted to do when I grew up. Once my childhood dreams of becoming a pastry chef or a dog whisperer slowly faded away, I searched for new interests!

During college, I challenged myself to take on different projects and internships ranging from data analysis, nonprofit consulting, and finally, product marketing.

I learned that I enjoy designing things, using data to analyze user behavior , and strategizing ways to resonate with audiences. I’m still open to new challenges, but for now, I’ve been obsessed with emerging technologies and how to integrate products into various ecosystems and services!
